Bargaining Minigames

Four standalone prototypes — nothing here touches the game
1 🌡️ Tension Meter

Nudge the price up push by push. Their face is your only gauge — and some customers bluff. Lock in before the third snap.

 2 ⏱️ Timing Bar

Push-your-luck reflexes. Stop the cursor in the zone to squeeze the offer higher; the zone shrinks every round. Bank or bust.

 3 📜 Dialogue Duel

Flatter, spin lore, or stand firm — each archetype falls for different patter, and lines wear thin when repeated. Learn the matrix.

 4 🪙 Coin Counter

The reverse haggle, made physical: drag coins onto the mat to build your offer while the seller's face reacts to the pile.

 5 🤝 Handshake Grip

One hold-to-squeeze seals the deal — longer grip means a richer price, but somewhere past their limit you crush their hand.

 6 ⚖️ Scale Balancing

Stack gold weights against the item; the wobbling beam shows how your ask sits against their hidden sense of fair.

 7 🎯 Coin Flick

Pay by slingshotting coins into their purse. Coins that skid off the table are gone — steady hands make cheap purchases.

 8 🎀 Gift Wrapping

The price is agreed; the tip isn't. Trace the parcel outline with ribbon — neat wrapping earns up to +30%, sloppy reopens the haggle.

 9 🍺 Pour the Ale

Deals are done over a pint. Hold to pour, mind the accelerating stream and settling head — land the fill line for the fattest signature.

 18 🔨 Auction Frenzy

Two buyers, one item, hidden ceilings. Goad them against each other in small or reckless steps — hammer before both walk.

 19 ⏳ Patience Hourglass

Their offer creeps up while they linger, but the sand drains at a hidden archetype speed. Stall with small talk — it wears thin.

 20 📖 Debt Ledger

Four weeks of collections. Gentle, firm, or threats per debtor — squeeze too hard and a regular is gone for good.
