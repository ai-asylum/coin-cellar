20 · Debt Ledger

Collection day — squeeze too hard and a regular never comes back
Collected: 0g
Four weeks of collections. Per debtor pick one approach: Gentle rarely collects but never offends · Firm is a coin-flip · Threaten nearly always collects but risks losing the regular for good. Unpaid debts grow 10% weekly and grudges deepen. Regulars lost stop generating new debts — final score is gold banked against customers kept.
